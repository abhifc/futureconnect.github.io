About Us


“Future Connect was promoted in year 2010 by a group of engineers having sound experience behind them in working with leading companies in the field of electronic security &Automation systems. This experience has helped us to offer the perfect customize solutions to the need.

We are proud to introduce ourselves as Importers, Distributors, System Designers & Integrators for a variety of electronic security and automation systems to enable on-line monitoring and controlling the access to various points, providing remote surveillance and perimeter protection.

Our Team

All our staff is well exposed to various applications and processes involved to offer perfect solutions. We have strong business and technical associations with the market leaders for their products. With the world-class quality products and our well-proven application knowledge, we are in a position to offer the most economical & techno-commercial solutions to customer’s needs.

Our Vision

To become solution selling company in the field of electronic security and automation field with 100% customer retention.

Our Approach

Safety &automation Redefined', being our objective, looks into key concerns such as Manageability and Flexibility for the optimum utilization of the best available systems & solutions in the field of security, with productive manpower and effective utilization and implementation.
